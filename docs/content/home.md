# Welcome to XOX

XOX by Dekideks is a simple tic-tac-toe iOS game focused on short matches and a clean, distraction-free experience.

## What you can do

- Play quick tic-tac-toe rounds.
- Change board size and win-condition settings.
- See rotating quotes in the app.
