# About XOX by Dekideks

XOX is an iOS board game app by Dexter Ramos (Dekideks).

## Product direction

- Keep gameplay fast and easy to understand.
- Avoid account setup friction.
- Iterate carefully based on user feedback.

## Audience

XOX is currently positioned for a **general audience** and is **not** listed in the App Store Kids Category.

## Future roadmap

Potential future updates may include:

- Additional game polish and UI customization.
- Monetization updates (for example, ads or premium features).
- Expanded settings and quality-of-life improvements.

If monetization or data processing changes, the Privacy Policy and App Store privacy details will be updated before release.
